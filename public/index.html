For job seekers

Showcase more of who you are with a GigCute Profile.

There are a lot of talented people with similar skills applying to the same job. Unfortunately, a résumé flattens you into bullet points and keywords, and it's hard to stand above the crowd.

A GigCute profile shows how you actually think: answer prompts in your own words, and highlight your projects, ambitions, and personality — letting you show how special and different you are from the rest!

Answer prompts that show who you are

Pick from dozens of prompts and answer in your own voice. Your answers sit right alongside your work history — the first thing a recruiter reads.

  • Stand out with substance, not keyword stuffing
  • Share a link to your GigCute profile on your sites.
  • Invite the recruiter/hiring manager to visit your GigCute profile.

How it works

Build it once. Share it everywhere.

Your GigCute profile is one link — and a QR code — that you can put anywhere a résumé or LinkedIn goes.

1

Build your profile

Answer prompts in your own voice — they mirror the questions you'd get in a phone screen or behavioral interview — then add your work, projects, education, certifications, and résumé.

2

Get your link & QR

Every profile gets a short, shareable link (like gigcute.com/profile/you) and a downloadable QR code — ready to drop anywhere.

3

Share it everywhere

Add the link to your résumé header, LinkedIn, and job applications. Use the QR on a business card or name tag at career fairs, or as a "scan me" slide in presentations and interviews.

Interview cheat sheet

The prompts provided are similar in theme to what you may talk about in an interview. Answer them once, store them on your profile, and print a cheat sheet to bring along whenever you need to prep.
